Guitar makers
for over two centuries.

After Uncle Jack crossed the river of Auroros and settled down
south of the green mountains, he established a guitar company
by the name of Lauenberg Guitars, after the city of his ancestors.
Ever since then, Lauenberg Guitars
has been the best in the guitar making.

Tradition and innovation.

All of our guitars are made here, locally
in the same shop where Uncle Jack made his first guitar.
Every instrument is handmade with tools used for generations.
While we strive for perfection we aren't afraid of using
new technologies and techniques.
