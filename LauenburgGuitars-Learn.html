Learn what makes our guitars so special.

Each guitar is handmade by our best luthiers.
To bring you the best sound and quality.

1. Quality wood.

Our journey starts in our local forest.
We sustain its ecosystem and harvest only the best quality wood from it.
We gather woods from spruce to mahogany.

2. Proper storage and selection.

Before a tree becomes a guitar, the wood needs to be selected, seasoned and dried.
This means that it needs to be stored for months and sometimes years,
before it can be suitable for use.

3. Luthiers hard at work.

Our best luthiers now transform the wood into works of art.
In the long process where the wood is cut, carved, shaped, glued, sanded and polished.
The tree becomes a guitar.

4. Final touches.

All guitars go through rigorous procedure of quality control.
Every guitar is tuned and adjusted by specialists to fit your needs.
